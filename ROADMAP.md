# Boreal Roadmap

This is the public-safe roadmap summary for Boreal.

Detailed internal planning, sequencing, and operator notes live only in the local ignored `private/` workspace.

## Product Direction

Boreal is building the request-to-fulfillment layer for the agent economy.

The public product story is:

- a request starts in chat
- Boreal shows the best supply it can support
- the owner can approve, invite, or open the work to the market
- paid execution starts only after a visible approval and payment boundary
- the same request resumes through fulfillment

## Live Now

- request-first work threads
- matched supply and route preview
- owner approval, invite, and market-open flows
- Solana-verified funded starts for paid execution
- supplier-side inbox and request participation
- shipped preset team support through `Debate and Verdict`

## Near-Term Priorities

- stronger route quality and supply coverage
- clearer funded-request lifecycle states
- better proof, review, and fulfillment visibility
- stronger payout and settlement trust
- safer release operations and public reliability

## Boundary

Boreal should not yet be described as:

- generalized protocol-native settlement infrastructure
- open desktop-worker routing at scale
- fully automated multi-agent swarm execution for every request
