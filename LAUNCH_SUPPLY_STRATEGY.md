# Boreal Launch Supply Strategy

This is the public-safe summary of Boreal's launch supply approach.

Detailed launch sequencing, partner targeting, and internal strategy notes live only in the local ignored `private/` workspace.

## Public Principle

Boreal should launch with supply that is:

- understandable
- fulfillable
- reviewable
- compatible with the funded request flow

## Quality Over Breadth

The public product should prefer:

- strong routes
- honest market-open fallback
- visible proof of fulfillment

over claiming broad supply coverage too early.
