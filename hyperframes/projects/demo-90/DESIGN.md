# Boreal Demo 90 Design

## Style Prompt

This cut should feel like the product proving itself under calm pressure.  It is not a cinematic fantasy layer over the app.  Dark graphite hook scenes can frame the story, but the proof moments must switch into Boreal's real light-first operating surface: near-white panels, charcoal type, cyan route signals, and restrained mint confirmation states.

## Typography

- `Syne` for big statements
- `Manrope` for explanatory and UI body copy
- `Geist Mono`, `IBM Plex Mono`, `monospace` for labels, route tags, and technical state

## Colors

- `#071311` deep graphite
- `#F7FAF9` near-white surface
- `#22C1F3` Boreal cyan
- `#62FFCE` mint confirmation
- `#EAF4F2` soft panel fill

## Rules

- no literal escrow claims
- no fake dark-mode product UI
- no decorative motion that is not carrying meaning
- use product nouns from the app: `Request`, `Supply`, `Proposal`, `Fulfillment`
