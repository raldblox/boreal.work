# Boreal Launch 60 Design

## Style Prompt

This cut should stop the scroll without pretending to be louder than the product.  The hook and close can sit on a dark graphite field, but the proof beats should use Boreal's actual light-first product language: clean surfaces, strong typography, cyan routing accents, and operational cards that feel like infrastructure rather than advertising.

## Typography

- `Syne` for the hook, audience cards, and brand close
- `Manrope` for supporting copy
- `Geist Mono`, `IBM Plex Mono`, `monospace` for labels and state language

## Colors

- `#071311` deep graphite
- `#F7FAF9` near-white surface
- `#EAF4F2` soft panel fill
- `#22C1F3` Boreal cyan
- `#62FFCE` mint confirmation

## Rules

- no escrow-release language in public proof scenes
- no pine-dark fake UI if the actual product surface is light
- no decorative motion without informational value
- keep the close to `public alpha` and `live today`, not stronger commercialization claims
