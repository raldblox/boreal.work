# Boreal Update 120 Design

## Style Prompt

This cut should feel like a founder walking peers through a real alpha, not a launch ad.  The scenes need enough editorial restraint to hold attention, but the center of gravity stays on product proof: light workspace surfaces, dense operational cards, and direct language about what runs today versus what still needs to be built.

## Typography

- `Syne` for headlines and section closes
- `Manrope` for body copy and UI detail
- `Geist Mono`, `IBM Plex Mono`, `monospace` for labels, roadmap items, and machine-state language

## Colors

- `#071311` deep graphite
- `#F7FAF9` near-white surface
- `#EAF4F2` soft panel fill
- `#22C1F3` Boreal cyan
- `#62FFCE` mint confirmation

## Rules

- no fake certainty about dates or milestones that the roadmap does not explicitly commit to
- no full on-chain escrow claims
- no dark-mode product recreations for proof scenes
- no hype language; keep the wording honest enough for other builders
