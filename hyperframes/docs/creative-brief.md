# Boreal HyperFrames Creative Brief

## Goal

Stand up a parallel Boreal video workspace in HyperFrames for HTML-native explainer cuts that can later mix:

- UI-inspired motion scenes
- real product captures from `next-app/`
- architecture overlays
- short variant renders for launch, investor, and technical audiences

## First Deliverable

`Boreal Explainer 48s`

This first cut should establish a practical narrative:

1. demand gets expressed and lost
2. Boreal turns the ask into a request
3. Boreal searches supply first
4. Boreal supports both direct fulfillment and open proposals
5. Boreal keeps the request accountable to the end

## Audience

- hackathon judges
- early users and operators
- future partners who need to understand the product quickly

## Creative Rules

- keep the story grounded in the current alpha
- use Boreal's actual product nouns and market language
- make the product logic legible in under a minute
- let graphics clarify routing rather than replace the product story

## Visual Rules

- cyan-forward, light-first
- editorial but operational
- no crypto-neon overload
- no generic SaaS dashboard gradients
- no hype-first language

## Variant Plan

- `Launch30`: compress to problem -> request -> close
- `Market45`: emphasize supply, profiles, and proposals
- `Technical60`: add service-provider and fulfillment overlays once capture assets exist
