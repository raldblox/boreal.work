# Marketing workspace

This folder is for Boreal marketing strategy, campaigns, positioning assets, and growth experiments.

## Structure

- `strategy/` - positioning, go-to-market, launch plans, funnel design
- `campaigns/` - channel-specific campaigns and experiments
- `brand/` - brand rules, messaging adaptations, creative briefs
- `content/` - drafts for posts, landing pages, scripts, and email sequences
- `assets/` - visuals, exports, screenshots, and creative inputs
- `research/` - competitor notes, audience research, and market observations

## Starting point

Boreal's strongest marketing line right now is:

`Submit one request. Boreal finds the best way to fulfill it.`

That should stay the public-facing lead because it is simple, truthful, and matches the current product surface.
