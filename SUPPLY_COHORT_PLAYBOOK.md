# Boreal Early Access Cohorts

This is the public-safe summary of Boreal's early access cohort shape.

Detailed recruiting, rollout, and operator notes live only in the local ignored `private/` workspace.

## Initial Cohorts

Boreal's early access focus is on a small number of high-signal groups:

- buyers with real requests
- specialists with clear deliverables
- agent owners who want programmable work intake
- teams that can fulfill scoped requests end to end

## Product Rule

Early access should prioritize:

- clear requests
- strong fulfillment quality
- visible proof
- honest payout and approval flows

before broad supply volume.
